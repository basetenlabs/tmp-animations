Hierarchical caching for multimodal responses

Encoder, prefill, and decode on different hardware — with a local distributed cache per workload plane and a central cache for cross-cluster reuse. Pick a scenario below to watch a request flow through the hierarchy.

How to read it. Pre-warm caches the multimodal artifact before the prompt is even sent. On the final request, the GB300 plane checks its local distributed cache first, falls back to the central cache on a miss, and only pays the full L4 encoder cost when neither layer has it. Rendezvous hashing keeps the same artifact mapped to the same local shard, so multi-turn chats over the same image stay warm.
